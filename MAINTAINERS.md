# Maintainers

This document lists the maintainers of the mockd project. Maintainers are responsible for the overall direction, quality, and health of the project.

|------|--------|------|------------|
| GetMockd Team | [@getmockd](https://github.com/getmockd) | Lead Maintainer | All |

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who have demonstrated a sustained commitment to the project may be invited to become maintainers. This typically includes:

- Consistent, high-quality contributions over an extended period
- Constructive participation in code reviews and issue discussions
- A strong understanding of the project's goals and architecture
- Demonstrated ability to collaborate respectfully with the community

To express interest in becoming a maintainer, start by becoming a regular contributor and engaging with the existing maintainers.

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are expected to:

- Review and merge pull requests in a timely manner
- Triage issues and help prioritize the roadmap
- Uphold the project's code quality and style standards
- Participate in release planning and execution
- Foster a welcoming and inclusive community
- Respond to security issues promptly and responsibly

Maintainers who are no longer able to fulfill these responsibilities should notify the team so arrangements can be made.
